My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that accounting and finance are not just subjects to be memorized, but practical tools that empower individuals to make informed decisions in both personal and professional life. My teaching approach combines theoretical knowledge with real-world applications, ensuring students understand not just the "what" but also the "why" behind financial concepts.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of guiding students from Class 9 through their Bachelor's degree programs. I take pride in adapting my teaching methods to suit different learning styles and academic levels, ensuring that every student can grasp complex financial concepts with confidence.

Beyond traditional classroom teaching, I am passionate about sharing insights through my blog, where I explore the intersection of accounting, finance, and technology. I believe in staying current with industry trends and incorporating modern tools and methodologies into my curriculum.
